Overview:

Tracking SC and DV clearances across a growing team shouldn't mean spreadsheets, chased emails, and hoping someone remembers before the audit. Vantage Gate is the single source of truth for every clearance your team holds with clear status, reminders before review dates lapse, and a full audit trail of who confirmed what, how, and when. So when a bid team asks "can we staff this contract?" or an auditor asks "show me the record for this person," you have the answer in one place.

Frequently Asked Questions:

Do you integrate with UKSV?
There is currently no such API that exists for third parties. So, Vantage Gate is where you record what you've confirmed from the official portals.
Do you run the checks?
No, we keep track for you. The Vetting happens through UKSV or your sponsoring organisation.
How is this different from other competitors?
Most companies are a vetting services firm with software attached. Vantage Gate is software only, built for firms that do their own sponsoring.
